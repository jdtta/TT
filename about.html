ABOUT

Jaipur District Table Tennis Association (JDTTA)

Jaipur District Table Tennis Association (JDTTA) is where champions are forged and dreams come alive. Affiliated with Rajasthan Table Tennis Association, District Olympic Association Jaipur, and District Sports Council Jaipur, JDTTA stands as a beacon for aspiring athletes who strive for excellence both on and off the table.

Here, every player is encouraged to push boundaries, embrace discipline, and unleash their potential. Our rigorous training programs, competitive tournaments, and supportive environment create a launchpad for future table tennis stars. We believe in the power of hard work, sportsmanship, and teamwork—values that turn passion into victory.

At JDTTA, every rally is a story of courage, every match a testament to perseverance, and every win a celebration of dedication. Our players inspire Jaipur, setting examples for the next generation and bringing pride to the city with every medal, handshake, and heartfelt cheer.

If you are driven by the spirit of sports, the thrill of competition, and the love for table tennis, JDTTA is your arena. Join us and play not just for points, but for legacy.

Our Mission

To inspire and nurture a passion for table tennis across Jaipur by providing opportunities for players of all ages and skill levels to learn, compete, and excel. We strive to build a vibrant sports community rooted in teamwork, integrity, and respect. By organizing events, coaching programs, and talent development initiatives, we aim to empower athletes, promote healthy lifestyles, and uphold the values of sportsmanship. Our association is committed to raising Jaipur’s stature in table tennis, supporting every journey from grassroots to championship, and fostering unity through the love of the game.

Our Vision

To become a leading force in table tennis, recognized for excellence, innovation, and inclusivity. We aspire to make Jaipur a prominent center for table tennis talent and culture, where enthusiasm for the sport inspires healthy living and lifelong friendships. By fostering skill development, providing world-class facilities, and encouraging community participation, we aim to shape champions who excel at every level and bring pride to our city and nation.
